Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?
No. Tekpair works directly from the web browser, on any device. No installations or manual updates.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes, without commitment or penalties. You can cancel your subscription whenever you want from your account panel.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is stored encrypted in the cloud, on EU servers and with automatic backups.
Can I migrate my data from another program?
Yes. Tekpair allows importing customers, stock and repairs from CSV files from other management programs.
Is there support in English?
Yes. Our support team speaks Spanish, English, French and Portuguese. Available via WhatsApp and email.
